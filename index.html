Why PawPanel

Built for dog owners who care deeply

Built by dog owners, for dog owners

PawPanel started because of two dogs — Soju, a mini Pomeranian, and Moutai, a Chow Chow. When our own dogs had unexplained symptoms, we couldn't find clear answers without expensive, stressful vet visits. So we built the tool we wished existed.

Science you can trust

Every result is processed through NATA-accredited laboratory partners and reviewed by licensed veterinarians before it reaches you. We don't cut corners on accuracy — your dog's health is too important.

Singapore-first

Designed specifically for Singapore pet owners, with local lab partnerships, local support, and an understanding of the breeds, climate, and diets most common here. We know your context because it's ours too.
