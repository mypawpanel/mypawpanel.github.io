## 7. FAQ Section

**Q: Do you just edit my resume or rewrite it completely?**
A: Full rewrite. I restructure, reword, and reformat from scratch based on your target role.

**Q: Will my resume pass ATS screening?**
A: Yes — I use clean formatting with no tables or graphics that confuse ATS systems, and include relevant keywords for your target role.

**Q: What if I don't have an existing resume?**
A: No problem. Just send me a bullet list of your experience and I'll build it from scratch.

**Q: How many rounds of revisions do I get?**
A: Depends on your package — 1 revision (Basic), 2 (Standard), 3 (Premium).

**Q: Do you offer rush delivery?**
A: Yes, I can deliver in 24 hours for an additional $20. Message me before ordering.
